Destination Review

Discovering the Magic of Kyoto

Kyoto, Japan's cultural heart, enchants visitors with its historic temples, cherry blossoms, and tranquil gardens.

  • Best time to visit: March to May, October to November
  • Must-see attractions: Fushimi Inari Shrine, Arashiyama Bamboo Grove
  • Local delicacies to try: Matcha Desserts, Kaiseki Cuisine
