Flaky Layered Parotta

Ingredients

  • 3 cups All-purpose flour (Maida)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 1 tablespoon Sugar
  • 1/4 cup Oil or Ghee
  • 1/2 cup Water (as needed for kneading)
  • Extra oil for layering

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, salt, and sugar in a large bowl.
  2. Gradually add water and knead the dough until soft and smooth. Add oil while kneading.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 1 hour.
  4. Divide the dough into equal-sized balls and roll each into thin sheets.
  5. Brush oil over the sheet and fold it like a fan, then roll it into a coil.
  6. Flatten each coil and roll it gently into a parotta shape.
  7. Cook on a hot skillet, flipping and brushing with oil until golden brown.
  8. Serve hot with curry or chutney of your choice!

Nutritional Information

  • Calories per parotta: 300-350 kcal
  • High in carbohydrates (from flour)
  • Contains healthy fats (from oil or ghee)

Fun Fact

Parotta is a popular South Indian street food, especially loved in Tamil Nadu and Kerala!
